About Me

Research at the intersection of physics & learning

I'm a PhD scholar at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), working on deep learning approaches to computational and seismic imaging. I'm fascinated by problems where data-driven models and classical physics-based reasoning meet — and where careful modelling translates directly into clearer images and better scientific insight.

🔬

Computational Imaging

Recovering high-fidelity images from indirect, incomplete, and noisy measurements using learned priors.

🧩

Inverse Problems

Combining optimization and deep networks to make ill-posed reconstruction problems stable and reliable.

🌊

Seismic & Geophysics

Applying machine learning to subsurface imaging — turning seismic data into interpretable structure.
